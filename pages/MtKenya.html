Topographic visualization of Mount Kenya

Introduction

This map is an experiment to explore different mapping techniques in ArcGIS Pro. It uses a hillshaded Digital Elevation Model (DEM) to bring out the mountainâ€™s rugged terrain, with colors transitioning from green (lowlands) to red (high-altitude peaks) for a clear sense of elevation.

What is on the Map?

  • Hillshade & DEM: This combination makes the landscape pop, showing ridges, valleys, and the overall shape of Mount Kenya.
  • Cartographic Experimentation: The goal was to try out different GIS tools and visualization techniques, just to see what works best.

Data Source

  1. The boundary data is from Protected Planet
  2. The digital Elevation Model(DEM) is from RCMRD
